Model DescriptionÔÉÅ

CN-WS consists of three major parts. A routing algorithm and two models: WaTEM/SEDEM (WS) and the Curve Number model (CN).

These parts are described in the subsequent chapters. The combination of the three parts is described in the chapter CN-WS, followed by a comprehensive overview of the used variables, formulas and their respective units.

Note

The Curve Number model within CN-WS can be used as an extension to WaTEM/SEDEM.
